WHAT SETS US APART

Crafting Tailored Elegance:
Where Your Soul Meets Excepcional Italian Design

OUR STORY

Established in the heart of Milano in 1982, our journey began with architect Hajj Chebaro’s unique vision to create timeless spaces that defied time beyond generations and trends. Since then, we've embarked on a journey that has taken us to over 50 cities worldwide, solidifying our position as curators in the realm of luxury Italian interior design.

We take pride in pioneering luxury interior design in Mexico and curating an exclusive collection of 'Made in Italy' brands. Collaborating closely with esteemed contemporary architects, we craft interior design high- end projects that grace some of the world's most extraordinary homes and hospitality spaces.

Our dedication shines a spotlight on the artistry of craftsmen and designers alike. In our two showrooms, spanning over 2,000 square meters, we offer and are an exclusive home to renowned brands such as Minotti, Rimadesio, Henge, Poltrona Frau, Exteta, and an impressive roster of 45 others. These spaces are more than showrooms; they are living testaments of our commitment to personalized luxury living.

OUR VALUE PROPOSITION

Designing spaces that are an extension of your soul, where every detail is a reflection of individuality and creativity. Driven by our passion for design and innovation, we empower you to craft tailored interiors that inspire and uplift, bringing harmony and serenity to life.

DESIGNLESS®

OUR PHILOSOPHY

The state of being beyond or unaffected by the constraints of time, where form and function exist not bound by the trends or styles of any particular era. Where spaces effortlessly harmonize with the past, present, and future.

Designless interiors is the embodiment of a space where the artistry of each era converges to create a symphony of timeless elegance. Here, the soul of a room is not confined by trends but is an enduring masterpiece, an inspiration for generations to come.

OUR GLOBAL PRESENCE

With over 40 years of experience, our story is a fusion of Italian elegance and global inspiration. It's a tale of innovation, where caring for every detail and every client's unique vision is paramount. With Milan as our birthplace, Mexico as our muse, and the world as our canvas, we invite you to explore the boundless possibilities of luxury living with us.

OUR EXCLUSIVE BRANDS

We take pride in pioneering luxury interior design in Mexico and curating an exclusive collection of 'Made in Italy' brands.

Minotti
was founded in 1948 by Alberto Minotti and has since become a world leader in high-end furniture. The brand's designs are characterized by their clean lines, minimalist aesthetics and attention to detail. Minotti collaborates with renowned international designers to create unique and innovative collections that push the boundaries of traditional furniture design. The brand's range of products includes sofas, armchairs, tables, beds and other accessories, all made with the best materials and attention to detail. Each piece is a work of art, carefully crafted to bring comfort, sophistication and style to any space.

Rimadesio
produces systems for the subdivision of environments and the architectural definition of interiors. Doors, sliding panels, bookcases and modular systems for the day area, dressing rooms and a collection of accessories: an offer in continuous expansion and constant stylistic evolution. Projects marked by concretion, in which the simplicity of the concept is elaborated through exceptional technical expertise. Rimadesio's objective is to continue developing its design culture and production technology, in order to offer functionally innovative proposals, capable of guaranteeing maximum reliability over time.

Henge
is known for its focus on experimentation and innovation in the production of high-quality furniture. The Henge philosophy is based on the idea of ​​creating designs that are unique and timeless, created to last a lifetime and beyond. The brand uses only high-quality materials such as marble, wood and glass, combining traditional production techniques with advanced technology to create exceptional pieces that appeal to design lovers from around the world. The brand has collaborated with some of the world's leading designers to create unique and exclusive collections that reflect their vision of modern and timeless design.

Exteta
draws inspiration from the Italian art of living to bring to life beautifully crafted and innovative designs. His Italian taste permeates every element, while the wisdom and experience of the Brianza region lend strength to his vision of excellence. The brand strives for quality in every element, capturing the inner essence of each material and presenting it to the world in a new way: this is its manifesto. Exteta's unique pieces are conceived for fluid and contemporary spaces. They adopt a new philosophy of life that no longer defines the environments by the limits between interiors and exteriors, but considers them hybrid spaces that must be brought to life with a creative eye.

Ernestomeda
For us, it is essential to respect ethical, ecological, and functional values, along with a constant policy of study and research: all of this is reflected in high-design projects. When we think about the kitchen, we also consider the environment in which it will be installed and pay attention to even the smallest details to ensure it integrates perfectly into your world. Only in this way is it possible to create something unique that is in harmony with space and time.

Poltrona Frau
is known for its luxury craftsmanship, and is considered one of the leading brands in the production of leather furniture. The brand uses a wide variety of high-quality leathers, from natural leathers to leathers that are treated and dyed to achieve a wide variety of colors and textures. Quality and craftsmanship are the pillars of the brand's philosophy, with each piece designed and produced by hand. In addition, Poltrona Frau has collaborated with some of the world's leading designers to create unique and innovative pieces that reflect their vision of modern design.
