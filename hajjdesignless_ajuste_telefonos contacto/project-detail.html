‍APARTMENT

This interior design project embodies a harmonious blend of contemporary sophistication and timeless elegance, offering unparalleled comfort in every detail. The open-plan layout seamlessly integrates the living room and dining area, creating an expansive and fluid environment. A neutral color palette of gray, white, and beige is enriched by wooden accents and metallic details, infusing warmth and modernity into the space. The wooden slat ceiling adds texture and depth, while sculptural lighting fixtures bring an artistic flair.

The dining area, highlighted by a long white marble table and light-toned upholstered chairs from Minotti, is the perfect setting for social gatherings and formal dinners, evoking a cozy and refined atmosphere for hosting guests. The adjacent living room features a comfortable modular sofa in light tones, complemented by brown leather accent chairs from Minotti, enhancing contrast and sophistication. The furniture arrangement promotes conversation and entertainment, with a low coffee table and a minimalist TV console that maintains the overall aesthetic.

Floor-to-ceiling windows flood the space with natural light and offer stunning exterior views, establishing a visual connection with the surroundings. Neutral-toned curtains provide a soft touch and balance light and privacy control.
