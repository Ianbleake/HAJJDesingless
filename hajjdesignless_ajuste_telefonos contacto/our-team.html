OUR FOUNDER

Hajj Chebaro's journey began in Lebanon, shaped by the challenges of growing up in a region marked by conflict. His path led him to Milan, where he earned a Doctorate from the Polytechnic of Milan, an experience that ignited his intellectual transformation, preparing him for his next chapter in Japan, where he spent five years taking and learning about the Japanese asthetic.

Following his time in Japan, he arrived in Mexico with a bold and visionary mission: to introduce the Mexican landscape to the artistry of Made in Italy design. He stood as a pioneer in this endeavor, being the first to unveil the concept of a brand showroom, allowing clients to intimately experience the intricacies of products as they were crafted in factories. His relentless pursuit of excellence and divine proportion was born from a deep understanding of life's complexities and challenges.

‍
This journey resulted in his emergence as a prominent figure, recognized for his ability to curate luxury with an innate understanding of the purest form of art. Hajj Chebaro built meaningful bridges between art and its appreciators. His personal journey of transformation is the cornerstone upon which his remarkable legacy stands.

LOS CHEBARO

Together, the Chebaro family transformed Hajj I Desingless into a global design powerhouse, specializing in crafting bespoke, high-end interiors for both residential and commercial clients. Each family member contributes their unique skills and expertise to deliver exceptional design services, sharing a deep dedication to craftsmanship, quality, and meticulous attention to detail. With the heartfelt commitment to personalization that characterizes Hajj, they take immense pride in creating beautiful, functional, and timeless spaces that artfully reflect their clients' distinct personalities and lifestyles, ensuring that every client feels uniquely cared for.
